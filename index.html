ðŸ“¬ Email-to-Ticket

Let's be blunt: Are you tired of training your team (again and again) on how to submit tickets? Dreading the hassle of installing yet another app on every workstation? BookMark is your answer.

No more complicated onboarding. With BookMark, you can turn any email into a trackable ticket instantly. Just set up our Email Listener in your settings. It will automatically scan the inbox of your chosen email address and convert every unread message into a ticket, ready for your team to triage and resolve.

Pro Tip: Use a dedicated email address for your tickets. Otherwise, you might accidentally flood your system with thousands of old emails!
  • Perfect for small businesses still using email or spreadsheets to track support requests.
  • Ideal for busy teams who don't have time for complex tools or endless training.
  • A lifesaver for HR or non-technical departments who just want to send an email and get help. No logins, no learning curve.
  • Seamless for everyone in between.

BookMark's Email-to-Ticket is the fastest way to bring order to your support chaos. No more lost requests, no more confusion. Just send, track, and resolve.
